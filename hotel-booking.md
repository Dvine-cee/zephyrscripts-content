ViserHotel is a complete hotel booking and room reservation solution built with PHP Laravel, perfect for anyone looking to launch a professional hotel business website. Guests can submit booking requests through the platform, which are managed and approved by the admin or receptionist by assigning available rooms. The system supports online payments and allows seamless handling of unlimited users, bookings, and reservation requests. With a responsive design and intuitive admin panel, ViserHotel is a ready-to-go solution that takes only minutes to set up—no need for costly development. Get your copy today and start building your hotel booking website with ease.


## Highlighted Features
All-in-One Hotel Booking & Management System  
- A powerful, Laravel-based solution to manage bookings, rooms, payments, guests, and more — all from a central dashboard.  

Online & Offline Room Booking  
- Accept and manage both digital and walk-in bookings with advanced approval and room assignment controls.

Multiple Room Booking with Merge Option  
- Book several rooms at once and merge them into a single invoice for better customer convenience.

Accept Payments Globally  
- Accept payments globally via 30+ payment gateways including cards, wallets, mobile money, crypto, or bank transfers with full flexibility.

Guest Checkout & Booking Confirmation 
- Let users book as guests with optional confirmation pages and offer coupon-based discounts.

Customizable Room Types & Extra Services  
- Configure amenities, bed types, complements, and add-on services with custom pricing and tax logic.

Multi-Role Access with Staff Permissions  
- Built-in role and permission system for admins, receptionists, and staff with tailored access control.

GDPR Compliant & Multi-Language Ready  
- Ensure privacy compliance and reach a global audience with full language translation support.

Push Notifications & Social Logins  
- Boost engagement and simplify access with instant alerts and social authentication via Google, Facebook, or LinkedIn.

Responsive, SEO-Optimized & Easy to Launch  
- Fast, mobile-friendly design with SEO tools and setup wizard—get your booking platform live in minutes.
